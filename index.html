About ILLUMINITS

ILLUMINITS, the Literary, Publications, and Fine Arts Society at NIT Silchar conducts a number of events under its yearly almanac. It presents great opportunity for individuals who are disposed to express their creativity and share their unique ideas and opinions.

It provides a space for people to celebrate and appreciate their artistic endeavours allowing storytellers to weave words together building dynamic tales, poets to unlock the ability to convey emotions in their rawest form, speakers to make a link between ideas and words and artists to infuse creativity, thought and imagination into tangible forms.

Our Mission

To cultivate a thriving community that champions the fusion of literature, publications, and fine arts as powerful mediums for creative expression. Through our three interconnected pillars, we seek to foster a sense of creativity, curiosity, and cultural appreciation.

Our Three Pillars

Literature

Promoting literary arts through discussions, workshops, and events that celebrate the written word in all its forms.

Publication

Creating platforms for sharing ideas through our annual magazine IGNITS and other publications.

Fine Arts

Nurturing artistic expression through various mediums and providing a platform for artists to showcase their talents.

Our Events

LPL

The annual literary fest of NIT Silchar that brings together literary enthusiasts from across the region.

IMLD

International Mother Language Day celebrations with various events promoting linguistic diversity.

Deprador & Kaladarshan

Art and cultural events that showcase the creative talents of our members.

Manthan

A series of discussions and debates on relevant topics that challenge conventional thinking.

Farewell

Celebrating and honoring our graduating members with special cultural programs.

Other Events

Regular workshops, open mics, and creative sessions throughout the year.
