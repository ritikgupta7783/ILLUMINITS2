How to Reach NIT Silchar

By Air

The nearest airport is Silchar Airport (IXS), located about 22 km from the campus.

By Train

Silchar Railway Station (SCL) is well-connected to major cities and is about 10 km from campus.

By Road

Silchar is connected by NH37 and NH54. Regular bus services are available from Guwahati and other cities.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join ILLUMINITS?

We conduct recruitment drives at the beginning of each academic year. Follow our social media channels and keep an eye on college announcements for recruitment details.

Do I need prior experience to join any club?

No prior experience is required for most clubs. We welcome enthusiasts who are passionate about literature, arts, and publications. We provide guidance and workshops to help you develop your skills.

Can I participate in events if I'm not a member?

Yes! Most of our events are open to all students of NIT Silchar. Some workshops or exclusive events might be for members only, but we'll always specify that in the event announcements.
